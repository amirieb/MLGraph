Syllabus and Textbooks

The required reading will be mainly from select journal/conference articles (listed below for each lecture), which are all available online. The following books are recommended as optional reading:

  • [GRL] Graph Representation Learning
    William L. Hamilton

  • [NCM] Networks, Crowds, and Markets: Reasoning About a Highly Connected World
    David Easley and Jon Kleinberg
